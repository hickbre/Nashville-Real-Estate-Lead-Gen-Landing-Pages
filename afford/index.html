Temporary buydown detail: Buydowns are funded by seller concession at closing. The loan note is always at the base rate; the concession subsidizes the buyer's payment during the buydown period. Lenders qualify borrowers at the base rate, not the temporary rate.

Annual Percentage Rate (APR): APR is not displayed in this tool. Actual APR varies based on lender fees, credit profile, loan terms, discount points, and market conditions, and will typically be higher than the note rate. Consult a licensed loan officer for the APR specific to your qualifying profile.
